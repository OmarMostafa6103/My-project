Once upon Alyaa

Authentic, creative, and mouthwatering. Alyaa will change everything you think you know about dates. Our mission is to elevate these ancient Egyptian fruits to artisanal delicacies. Alyaa was founded by Gannate Khowailed, Ceren Gokoglu & Mona El-Halwagy, women with a passion for elevating local agriculture and farmers to a global audience. They always believed there was more potential for dates and Alyaa is their mission to fulfill Egyptian dates potential. We invite you to experience the hand-crafted gourmet enchantment of Alyaa and try the remaining Foodiena brands.

Local Spirit Global Spin

We reflect the true Egyptian spirit; a perfect harmony of tradition and innovation. We harvest the finest, highest-quality dates across Egypt to deliver only the most exceptional tasting products, all while supporting local farmers and small businesses. Your purchase directly impacts the families who help grow and produce Alyaa dates.

Luxury means one of a kind

Creativity and sophistication define everything we do. Alyaa is a multi-sensory experience you have to feel to believe. From unexpected products, flavors, and design, Alyaa is here to surprise and delight the senses.
